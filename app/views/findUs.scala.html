via Tram

QA Cinemas is easily accessible via the tram.
Metrolink offers fast,frequent and fully accessible transport to and from Manchester city centre.
Take the Eccles line to MediaCityUK and walk across the footbridge towards the Lowry Outlet.

Via Train

To reach QA Cinemas by train, first take the train to Manchester Piccadilly or Manchester Victoria Station, then take the tram stop nearby, then follow the instruction to get to QA Cinemas Via Tram.
